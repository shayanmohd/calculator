# Privacy Policy

Effective date: May 5, 2026

Minimalist Calculator is a calculator app with an optional private media vault feature. This Privacy Policy explains how the app handles user data.

## Data collection

Minimalist Calculator does not collect, sell, rent, or share personal information with third parties.

The app does not use analytics SDKs, advertising SDKs, tracking SDKs, or third-party backend services.

## Media files

When you import photos or videos into the vault, the selected files are processed locally on your device.

Imported vault media is:

- Stored inside the app's private internal storage
- Encrypted before being saved by the app
- Not uploaded to any server by this app
- Not shared with third parties by this app

The app uses Android's system file picker to let you choose files. It does not request broad external storage access.

## Passcode information

The calculator tap-sequence passcode is stored locally on your device in a protected derived form. The app does not transmit the passcode to any server.

If you forget the passcode, there is no remote recovery system. Clearing app data or uninstalling the app will remove the stored passcode and vault data.

## Temporary preview cache

To preview encrypted vault files, the app may temporarily decrypt media into the app's private cache area. These preview files remain inside the app sandbox and are cleaned by the app during normal use.

## Device permissions

Minimalist Calculator is designed to avoid broad storage permissions. File selection is handled through Android's document picker.

The app may use standard Android platform features such as:

- App-private storage
- Android Keystore
- Secure window flags to reduce screenshots and recent-app previews where supported

## Backups

The app disables Android backup and data extraction rules for app data where supported. This helps prevent vault content from being included in normal Android cloud backup or device-transfer flows.

## Data sharing

Minimalist Calculator does not intentionally share user data with external services.

## Data deletion

You can delete vault items inside the app. You can also remove all app data by uninstalling the app or clearing app storage from Android settings.

Deleting or clearing app data removes the stored passcode and vault files from the app's private storage.

## Children's privacy

Minimalist Calculator is not directed at children under 13. The app does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

## Security limitations

Minimalist Calculator is designed to improve privacy for normal device use, but no app can guarantee absolute protection. Rooted devices, compromised operating systems, malware, physical access to an unlocked device, or advanced forensic tools may weaken security protections.

## Changes to this policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated if the app's data handling changes. Updates will be published with the app or in the project repository.

## Contact

For privacy questions, contact the app developer or organization that publishes the app on Google Play.
